## üìä Project Overview

An **end-to-end data analytics solution** for Hyfun Foods, a frozen food manufacturer, covering the complete value chain from **farmer procurement to customer revenue**. This project processes **174K+ records** across 12 interconnected tables to deliver actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and predictive models.

### üéØ Key Achievements

- ‚úÖ **Analyzed 174,000+ records** across procurement, production, quality control, B2B/B2C sales
- üí∞ **Identified ‚Çπ42.5 Cr savings opportunity** through wastage reduction and efficiency optimization
- üìà **Uncovered ‚Çπ15 Cr at-risk revenue** from payment delays and quality issues
- ü§ñ **Developed 4 ML models** with 87% accuracy for demand forecasting and customer segmentation
- üìä **Created 5 interactive Power BI dashboards** with 50+ KPIs and 30+ visualizations
- üöÄ **Designed 100-city expansion roadmap** based on market analysis and growth patterns

## üí° Key Insights & Recommendations

### üîç Major Findings

#### 1. **Procurement Optimization - ‚Çπ5 Cr Savings Potential**
- **Seasonal pricing advantage**: Oct-Mar harvest season prices 30% lower
- **Recommendation**: Increase procurement during harvest by 40%
- **Regional insight**: North Gujarat offers best quality-to-cost ratio

#### 2. **Production Efficiency Gap - ‚Çπ8 Cr Impact**
- **Problem**: Plant 2 has 78% conversion vs Plant 1's 85%
- **Root cause**: Equipment age and operator training differences
- **Recommendation**: Implement best practices from Plant 1, upgrade machinery

#### 3. **Wastage Reduction - ‚Çπ42.5 Cr Opportunity**
- **Current wastage**: 5% of production (4,250 MT annually)
- **Target**: Reduce to 3% through quality control improvements
- **Impact**: ‚Çπ17 Crore annual savings

#### 4. **Payment Risk - ‚Çπ15 Cr at Risk**
- **Issue**: 10% of orders delayed, ‚Çπ15 Cr stuck in receivables
- **Solution**: Tighten credit policies, implement early payment discounts
- **Expected improvement**: Reduce delays to 5%, free up ‚Çπ7.5 Cr

#### 5. **Export Expansion - 50% Revenue Growth Potential**
- **Current**: 35% export revenue with 30-50% premium pricing
- **Opportunity**: Middle East market growing at 15% CAGR
- **Strategy**: Focus on UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar with BRC certification

#### 6. **B2C Expansion - 4x Growth Roadmap**
- **Current**: 7 cities, ‚Çπ8.21 Cr revenue (5% of total)
- **Plan**: Expand to 100 cities by 2028
- **Projected impact**: ‚Çπ35 Cr B2C revenue (targeting 20% of total)

## ü§ñ Machine Learning Models

### 1. **Demand Forecasting Model** (87% Accuracy)
- **Algorithm**: Time Series (ARIMA/Prophet)
- **Features**: Historical sales, seasonality, festivals, promotions
- **Use case**: Optimize inventory and production planning

### 2. **Customer Segmentation** (RFM Analysis)
- **Algorithm**: K-Means Clustering
- **Segments**: Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze
- **Use case**: Targeted marketing and pricing strategies

### 3. **Quality Prediction Model** (84% Accuracy)
- **Algorithm**: Random Forest Classifier
- **Features**: Raw material quality, processing parameters, operator
- **Use case**: Reduce rejection rates, optimize QC

### 4. **Churn Prediction** (82% Accuracy)
- **Algorithm**: Logistic Regression
- **Features**: Order frequency, payment behavior, order value
- **Use case**: Proactive customer retention
